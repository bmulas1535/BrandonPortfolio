Why Data Science?

(And what it means to me)

Data Science is the science and art of taking in noise, and deciphering a meaning. Finding trends in data is nothing new, to be sure. But the science behind it has evolved, and continues to evolve.

Now we can take a several thousands year old complicated concept and using it to answer a present day question. Expanding old ideas with new technology is how we throttle the engines of progress. Being a Data Sceintist lets me be part of that ride.

Where I Stand

My mission is to ultimately bring Data Science, and the logic used therein, to bear against some of the toughest problems facing humanity today. The field has contributed much to corporations seeking the best ad marketing campaign, or political forecasting. What I desire is to use those same skills to discover ways to make life more fulfilling, and make knowledge accessible to all who seek it.

My Personal Goals

It's important to consider that any machine learning algorithm is only as good as the intuition provided in it's application.
